Leave Your Mark

Leave Your Mark is a nonprofit committed to empowering the youth by encouraging them to express their opinions about global events.
×

Leave Your Mark was founded by Maria and Patricia; they have been friends since 5th grade. The nonprofit has held meetings since autumn, with the objective of implementing a plan to two schools in Jersey City. The goal is to have a series of workshops on a bimonthly basis beginning in Sept. covering topics like the election and global warming.
